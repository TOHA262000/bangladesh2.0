Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving progress through technology, research, and entrepreneurship. Bangladesh 2.0 promotes creative solutions, AI adoption, and a startup culture that transforms local challenges into global opportunities

Sustainability

Building a future that balances growth with environmental responsibility. We support renewable energy, climate resilience, and sustainable development that protects the next generation.

Infrastructure

Creating modern systems that power a digital nation. Smart cities, reliable transport, strong connectivity, and resilient energy networks form the backbone of Bangladesh 2.0.

Empowerment

Empowering people through education, digital access, and equal opportunity. When citizens are informed, skilled, and connected, national progress becomes unstoppable.
